# EduCoin 2.0: The Final Master Build Specification ## 1. Project Vision EduCoin 2.0 is a "Digital Observatory" ecosystem where academic discipline is converted into a liquid economy. It bridges the gap between study habits and real-world value through AI-powered focus tracking, parent-guided incentives, and a high-stakes gamified social layer. ## 2. Core Technical Architecture - **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion (for smooth transitions/confetti). - **Backend:** Node.js, Express, TypeScript, Socket.io (Real-time). - **Database:** PostgreSQL + Prisma (Complex relations for P2P trading). - **AI Engine:** TensorFlow.js + BlazeFace for local-only focus detection. ## 3. Essential User Flows & Screens ### A. The Student Journey 1. **The Observatory (Dashboard):** Real-time stats, levels, and study streaks with a futuristic HUD. 2. **Quantum Lab (Study Session):** AI Focus tracking, live coin accumulation, and local camera anti-cheat. 3. **Knowledge Nexus (Library):** Personalized learning resources shared by parents or discovery. 4. **The Global Market (Store):** Buy/Sell digital skins, exchange coins for real money/gift cards. 5. **Transmissions (Social Hub):** Direct P2P chat, "Collectives" (Classes), and real-time activity feeds. ### B. The Parent Authority 1. **Command Center (Dashboard):** Monitor all children's live status, focus scores, and alerts. 2. **Treasury & Payouts:** Manage funding, automatic goal-based rewards, and impact analysis. 3. **Curation Hub (Library):** Assign readings and track family-wide trending subjects. 4. **Control Panel:** Granular restrictions on store access, study goals, and social permissions. ## 4. Gamification: "The EduSkeleton" System (NEW) - **Concept:** Every student has a 3D "Online Skeleton" avatar visible in the Social Hub and Leaderboards. - **Skins:** High-rarity skins (Legendary, Epic) inspired by PUBG/Modern RPGs (e.g., "Neon Hacker Bone", "Golden Scholar Frame"). - **Economy:** - Skins cost significant EduCoins or real currency. - **Resale Market:** Students can sell skins back to the system for 70% value or trade/sell to friends. - **Appreciation:** Limited edition skins gain value over time based on demand. ## 5. Financial Innovation: P2P & Bi-Directional Tipping (NEW) - **Split Among Friends:** "Study Groups" can split coin rewards or pool funds for "Class Prizes." - **Student-to-Parent Tips:** Students can "tip" parents back with "Gratitude Coins" (which might unlock parent-side perks or just act as a social signal). - **Observatory Node:** Direct exchange of EduCoins to USD/PayPal via the secure marketplace. ## 6. Visual Identity: Lumina Nexus - **Color Palette:** Deep Indigo (#0F0F13) Base, Electric Purple (#6C5CE7) for Students, Cyber Teal (#00CEC9) for Parents. - **Typography:** Space Grotesk (Headers), Inter (Body). - **Vibe:** Sleek, high-contrast, data-driven, and empowering.
